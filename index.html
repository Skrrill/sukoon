Questions

Good to know.

Do I need any equipment?

No. Sessions are built around bodyweight movement, so there's nothing to buy. If you already own a mat or a couple of light items, we can use them, but it's never required.

Which parts of Bengaluru do you cover?

In-home sessions are available across Bengaluru. Online sessions are available anywhere in the world, on your schedule. Message me and I will confirm timing.

How does payment work?

Pay per session or save with a monthly pack of 8 sessions. UPI or cash, whatever's easier for you. No long contracts and no joining fee.

What should I expect in the first session?

We start gentle. The first session is about understanding your goals, your comfort level, and how your body moves, then building a plan from there. Wear something comfortable and have a little open floor space ready.

Can I reschedule?

Yes. Life happens. Just give a little notice on WhatsApp and we'll find another time that works.

How often should I train?

Most people do around two sessions a week, which is what the monthly pack is built for. We can go lighter or heavier depending on your goals and schedule.
